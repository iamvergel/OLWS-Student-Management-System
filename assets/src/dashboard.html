    Upcoming School Activities

    Fun-Filled Activities Ahead for Elementary Students!

    Dear Parents and Guardians,

    We are thrilled to announce an array of exciting activities tailored specifically for our elementary school students! These events promise to ignite curiosity, spark creativity, and foster a sense of joy and camaraderie among our young learners. Here's a glimpse of what's in store:

    • Science Exploration Day: Prepare for a day of wonder and discovery as our young scientists delve into the fascinating world of science! Through interactive experiments and hands-on demonstrations, students will explore various scientific concepts while having a blast.
    • Literacy Adventure Week: Let the adventure begin! During Literacy Adventure Week, students will embark on an exciting journey through the pages of their favorite books. From character dress-up days to storytelling sessions, this week-long celebration of literacy promises to ignite a love for reading and storytelling.
    • Sports Carnival: Get ready to cheer on our budding athletes at the Sports Carnival! From relay races to friendly competitions, students will showcase their sportsmanship and teamwork while participating in a variety of fun-filled activities. It's a day of active play and healthy competition that promises to leave everyone with a smile on their face.
    • Arts and Crafts Extravaganza: Unleash your creativity at our Arts and Crafts Extravaganza! Students will have the opportunity to explore their artistic talents through a variety of hands-on craft activities. From painting and sculpting to collage-making, there's something for every budding artist to enjoy.
    • Community Service Project: Making a difference, one act of kindness at a time! Our students will come together to participate in a special community service project aimed at spreading kindness and compassion. Whether it's cleaning up a local park or collecting donations for those in need, this project reinforces the importance of giving back to our community.

    We can't wait to see our students engage, learn, and grow through these enriching experiences. Stay tuned for more details on each event and how you can get involved!

    Warm regards,
    Kadusale, Jenalyn
